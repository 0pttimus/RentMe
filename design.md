# MyTonWallet Redesign Instructions

## Goal
Redesign the app to feel like a premium crypto wallet with a quiet, dense, highly polished interface. The result should feel secure, technical, and expensive rather than flashy or playful.

## Visual Direction
- Use a near-black outer canvas with layered dark gray panels.
- Keep one strong blue accent and only a few restrained status colors.
- Favor soft separators over heavy borders.
- Use rounded rectangles with a controlled, uniform radius.
- Avoid bright gradients, glow effects, oversized shadows, and decorative backgrounds.
- Make every surface feel intentional, compact, and functional.

## Color System
- App background: almost black.
- Primary surface: very dark charcoal.
- Secondary surface: deeper black or near-black.
- Accent: saturated blue.
- Success: muted green.
- Warning or error: muted red.
- Primary text: bright white.
- Secondary text: cool gray.
- Tertiary text: softer gray.
- Dividers: translucent white or blue-gray with low opacity.

## Layout
- Use a master-detail layout on desktop.
- Keep a narrow left sidebar for navigation and account switching.
- Use a large content panel on the right with card-based sections.
- Keep rows dense and easy to scan.
- Keep page chrome minimal so the content feels centered and calm.
- On mobile, collapse to stacked panels with smooth slide transitions.

## Cards and Surfaces
- Use cards for wallets, assets, actions, and settings sections.
- Keep cards low elevation with subtle contrast.
- Use a consistent radius across the app.
- Keep padding disciplined and repeatable.
- Separate sections with spacing and soft rules, not heavy outlines.
- Use one dominant card style so the app feels coherent.

## Typography
- Use a clean sans-serif with strong legibility.
- Keep titles compact and slightly bold.
- Make secondary text smaller and cooler in tone.
- Align numeric values carefully.
- Use whitespace to make balances and percentages easy to scan.
- Avoid decorative or expressive type choices.

## Iconography and Assets
- Use custom app-specific icons, token logos, and chain artwork.
- Do not rely on generic UI icon sets for the main visual identity.
- Use sharp, high-resolution assets.
- Keep illustrations minimal and product-specific.
- Use animated assets only when they add clarity or state feedback.

## Motion
- Animate navigation with smooth 250 to 350 ms transitions.
- Use slide and fade motion, not bouncy motion.
- Keep motion restrained and purposeful.
- Animate panels, drawers, and detail views more than tiny controls.
- Preserve a strong sense of stability while content changes.

## Buttons and Controls
- Use rounded controls with clear hierarchy.
- Primary actions should be blue and visually distinct.
- Secondary actions should be subdued.
- Destructive actions should be red but not loud.
- Keep hover states subtle.
- Make toggles, dropdowns, and segmented controls feel compact and precise.

## Data Presentation
- Show balances, asset lists, and status changes in dense rows.
- Use small badges for APR, growth, and chain labels.
- Keep positive and negative states readable but restrained.
- Align numbers cleanly.
- Avoid charts or visual noise unless they support a decision.

## Interaction Feel
- The app should feel fast, secure, and calm.
- Feedback should be immediate but not flashy.
- Loading states should be minimal and elegant.
- Empty states should be quiet and informative.
- Confirmation flows should feel careful and trustworthy.

## Responsive Behavior
- Desktop should feel like a full control surface.
- Tablet and mobile should preserve hierarchy without bloating spacing.
- Keep touch targets usable, but do not inflate the layout into a generic mobile app look.
- Maintain the same visual identity across platforms.

## What To Avoid
- Avoid generic fintech templates.
- Avoid glassmorphism.
- Avoid bright white backgrounds.
- Avoid purple-heavy palettes.
- Avoid oversized hero sections.
- Avoid playful illustration styles.
- Avoid excessive card shadows or floating panels.
- Avoid stock app spacing and typography.

## Implementation Summary
- Build the app as a dark, premium, token-driven crypto operating surface.
- Use compact cards, disciplined blue accents, precise typography, and smooth restrained motion.
- Keep the design consistent across desktop, iOS, and Android.
